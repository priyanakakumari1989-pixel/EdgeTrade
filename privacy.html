Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

EdgeTrade ("we", "our", "us") operates a free trading journal platform at edge-trade-sigma.vercel.app. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: name, email address, and encrypted password when you register.
  • Trading data: trades you log manually or that sync from a connected broker — entry/exit price, direction, P&L, strategy, psychology notes, and screenshots you upload.
  • Broker API credentials: if you connect a broker, your API key and secret are encrypted (AES-GCM) before storage. We request read-only permissions only and never place, modify, or cancel trades on your behalf.
  • Usage data: basic technical logs (device type, browser) for debugging and security.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate your journal — display trades, calculate statistics, and sync broker data.
  • To power the AI Assistant, AI Statistics Analyst, and AI Calculator features, which analyze your journal data (and, if you choose to upload one, a chart screenshot) to generate insights. These features never receive your broker API keys.
  • To maintain account security and prevent unauthorized access.

3. How We Store Your Data

Data is stored using Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security, meaning your data is only accessible to your own authenticated account. Broker API keys are encrypted at rest and decrypted only in memory, server-side, when syncing trades.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and backend functions.
  • Vercel — website hosting.
  • Broker/Exchange APIs (Binance, Delta Exchange, Zerodha, etc.) — read-only trade history sync, only for brokers you explicitly connect.
  • AI providers (e.g. Google Gemini, Groq) — process journal text you submit to the AI Assistant/Analyst to generate responses. No broker credentials are ever sent to these providers.
  • TradingView — charting library used to display market charts.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can disconnect a broker or delete your account at any time from Settings; this permanently removes your stored trades and credentials.

6. Your Rights

You may access, correct, export, or delete your data at any time through the app, or by contacting us.

7. Cookies

We use minimal essential cookies/local storage strictly for session authentication — no third-party advertising trackers.

8. Children's Privacy

EdgeTrade is not directed at individuals under 18 and we do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as EdgeTrade evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with an updated date.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out via the in-app Support option.
